About


Data Fixers is a data-driven journalism project sponsored by The Brown Institute for Media Innovation – a joint initiative between Columbia University and Stanford University. We received a Magic Grant to find data and public records to investigate environmental crimes in the Brazilian Amazon.

Send an email if you want to hire us or are interested in making a partnership!
