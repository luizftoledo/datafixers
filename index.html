About


Data Fixers is a data-driven journalism project sponsored by The Brown Institute for Media Innovation, at Columbia and Stanford universities. This initiative received a Magic Grant in 2022 to find data and public records that help explain environmental crimes in Brazil. If you want us to want to write stories about environmental crime or need help building your project, contact us!
